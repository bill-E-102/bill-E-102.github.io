2. Frame Definitions

Recall a frame is tight if

\sum_{i=1}^M \langle x, f_i\rangle\,f_i = \frac{M}{n}\,x,\quad \forall\,x\in\mathbb{C}^n.
3. Welch Bound $$ \max_{i\neq j} \bigl|\langle f_i, f_j\rangle\bigr| \;\ge\; \sqrt{\frac{M-n}{n(M-1)}}. $$
