Grassmannian Codes from Stratified Frames

In this thesis we study unit‐norm tight frames $\{f_i\}_{i=1}^M\subset\C^n$ stratified by coherence. Our objective is to show each stratum attains near‐optimal Grassmannian packings:

$$ \max_{i\neq j}|\langle f_i, f_j\rangle| \;\ge\; \sqrt{\frac{M-n}{n(M-1)}}. $$

Recall a frame is tight if

\begin{equation*} \sum_{i=1}^M \langle x, f_i\rangle f_i = \tfrac{M}{n}\,x, \quad \forall x\in\C^n. \end{equation*}
